# Decoding Customer Value: A SQL-Driven Retention Strategy

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes a dataset of approximately 3,900 customers to transform raw transactional data into actionable intelligence[ci. The primary objective is to shift the brand's approach from a reactive, promotion-heavy sales model to a sustainable, loyalty-first retention strategy. 

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* **Data Processing & Feature Engineering:** Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* **Database Management & Analysis:** SQLite
* **Data Visualization & Modeling:** Power BI

## 📊 Methodology & Data Pipeline
* **Feature Engineering:** Created custom metrics such as `Promo_Hunter`, `Estimated_LTV` (Lifetime Value), and `Value_Tier` to segment customers.
* **Loyalty Definition:** Established precise loyalty labels based on purchase frequency, number of previous purchases, and subscription status.
* **Database Integration:** Loaded the finalized dataset into a local SQLite database (`d2c_database.db`) to perform advanced aggregations and extract strategic insights.

## 💡 Key Findings
* **Segmented Loyalty:** A significant portion of revenue is driven by "Promo Hunters," whereas a core segment of "High-Tenure" customers exhibits strong organic demand.
* **Category Dynamics:** Category preference analysis indicates that "Clothing" and "Footwear" act as primary anchors for long-term customer retention.
* **Geographic Opportunity:** Geographic data reveals specific regions with high organic traction that have been under-leveraged in marketing, offering a clear opportunity for optimized budget allocation.

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations (Retention Playbook)
* **Promo Sunset Plan:** Systematically reduce reliance on discounts by replacing broad-spectrum promotions with targeted, value-based rewards for "High-Tenure" and "Mid-Tenure" segments.
* **Budget Reallocation:** Maximize Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) by shifting advertising budgets from discount-heavy segments to regions identified as having high organic potential.
* **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Targeting:** Optimize future acquisition campaigns to target demographic profiles that mirror the "High-Tenure" segment, specifically focusing on users with higher satisfaction and lower discount sensitivity.
